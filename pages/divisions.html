The Divine Immortals Divisions

The Exiled Infantry

Summery:

Generalist Military

Overview:

The Exiled Infantry Division is meant to be the “general Clan” membership, and is designed to support the largest population. The staff housed in this Division are general-purpose Clan-activity staff.

This is also the exclusive Division of non-combat staff such as MDS and FAS.

MOS minimum requirements:

  • BCT Certified
  • Event Attendance (Grade I)
  • Uniform Certified

    • Staff in this Branch:

  • Media Staff
  • Affairs Staff
  • Recruiting Staff

    • Rank Structure:

  • E1G1 - E5G4
  • E6G1 - E9G4
  • W1G1 - W5G3
  • O1G1 - O4G2

    The Red Regiment

    Summery:

    Lower Rung Special Forces

    Overview:

    The Red Regiment Division is designed primarily to house incredible raiders and/or Roleplay-specific operations groups. Raid-specific game types, typically of unlimited time, are the primary jurisdiction of special forces. Naturally, this group will interact most with the other Divisions, as the very high skill ceiling of the Chimera Corps. Division will be useful for operations that require as such, and the large forces and long-term experience of TEI will serve as manpower for larger-scale operations. Regardless, TRR marries the idea of high skill with high utility for direct action groups.

    The Apollo Watch

    Summery:

    The Security Staff

    Overview:

    The Apollo Watch is the group within TDI that oversees the discipline and professionalism of TDI. TAW does not openly recruit. The members are chosen by the Royal Council or Officers of TAW. They then go through a long boot camp as well as varying other training sessions. The TAW ranks are considered a "tier up" than the standard ranks. Ex. A TAW NCOs word will be taken over the standard NCO. There are also no grades within TAW.

    Security in meetings and for VIP's, Regulation moderation in game and on discord, and military Police. They are the Direct Path to Chimera Corps due to them being the most disciplinary side of the clan.

    They are expected to be highly skilled and incredibly professional.

    MOS minimum requirements:

  • Security (Introductory)
  • BCT Certified
  • ACT Certified
  • CSR (Grade II)

    • Staff in this Branch:

  • Security Staff
  • Discipline Staff

    • Rank Structure:

  • E1G1 - E5G4
  • E6G1 - E9G4
  • W1G1 - W5G3
  • O1G1 - O4G2
  • Chief of Security
  • Admiral of Security
